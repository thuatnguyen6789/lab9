FIXED GEAR FOREVER

16 APRIL 2021

The veloheld combines
minimalist design with
superb quality. Devoid of
excessive graphics and gear
shift components, the
veloheld product range
delights us with its beauty
and simplicity. The black
and white (yin and yang?)
bicycles feature a short
wheelbase, a single gear
and a narrow handlebar...
All you need to explore the
city streets.

For those who want to
create their bike
themselves, the veloheld
frames come in three sizes
and two colours and are the
perfect starting point.
